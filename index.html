Welcome to SNDT ACM-W

Founded in 1999, the SNDT-ACM Student Chapter was the first ACM chapter established at SNDT Women’s University in Mumbai.
For over 25 years, the chapter has been dedicated to fostering technical competence among students in the fields of Computer Science,
Engineering, and Management, while also aiming to uplift and inspire women to excel in these disciplines.
As we mark a significant milestone in February 2024, the chapter remains committed to cultivating leadership qualities
and managerial skills, empowering its members to make meaningful contributions to both the technological and professional domains.

About Us

Who are we?

SNDT ACM-W Student Chapter is dedicated to advancing and empowering women in computing and tech. Through mentorship, networking opportunities, and professional development programs, SNDT ACM-W provides the resources and support necessary for women to succeed in the tech industry. We celebrate and highlight the achievements of women making significant contributions to computing.

What we do?

SNDT ACM-W Student Chapter fosters a supportive environment where women can thrive in computing and technology. We organize workshops, events, and initiatives to build leadership skills, technical knowledge, and confidence. Our aim is to bridge the gender gap in the tech industry by encouraging women to take on leadership roles and excel in their careers.
